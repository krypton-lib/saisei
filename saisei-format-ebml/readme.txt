EBML
---
a kotlin multi-platform ebml[1] parser. it's not really based off of anything, but it does draw some
ideas/inspirations from jebml[2] & lavaplayer[3]

Support?
---
feel free to join our discord server: https://discord.gg/8R4d8RydT4

Examples?
---
if you need an example (which you probably will) feel free to look at our mkv reader.
